Speed Hunters

Sharp yellow eyes, covered in darkness, were staring down at the young boy as he stood there all alone. The young boy had pitch-black hair that covered his eyes and was dressed in clothes that was just as black. The eyes kept staring the boy, as time slowly went by and they both were silent.

“Tell me young boy”

The dark figure finally asked the boy.

“What is a beginning?”

“Is it the first cry of a newborn child? The first chapter of a story? When a new life starts to bloom?”

“What is an end?”

“When the flower withers away? A man’s last breath? The happily ever after?”

“None of these are correct.”

“Before the beginning, there were the people who gave birth to you, natural occurrences that grew the forest, the very inspiration of creation.”

“After the end, the fact that you lived will affect the future, the readers of the story become influenced, and a single dead tree continues its existence in the forest.”

“If we were to follow that theory, then it would be safe to say that this story, we are in, didn’t begin with the beginning and won’t end with the ending.”

“It began with the end and ended with the beginning.”

The boy remained silent, as he looked upon the devastated wasteland around him that revealed the end and spoke of a beginning.
